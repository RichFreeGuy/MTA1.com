Moon Heist: The Game

Welcome to the Moon!

Moon Heist is an open-world adventure game set on the Moon where gravity feels just like Earth's. Explore futuristic lunar cities inside massive glass domes, embark on missions, and engage in space heists as you travel across a colonized Moon!

Take control of a variety of vehicles designed for low-gravity environments, face off against rogue astronauts, and experience a sci-fi adventure like no other. The lunar surface is your playground, and only you can decide your path.

Game Features

  • Open-world map of the Moon with Earth-like gravity
  • Futuristic lunar cities, space outposts, and underground tunnels
  • Real-time heist missions involving lunar cargo ships and bases
  • Vehicles designed for lunar travel, including hover bikes and space rovers
  • Compatible with both mobile and PC
